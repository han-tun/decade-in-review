DATA & METHODOLOGY

Using the New York Times Article Search API, I downloaded all articles from 2010 - 2019. I then aggregated the number of citations (recorded as "tags" in the NYT metadata) for every month, borken down by the category of articles they belonged to. The New York Times has a "Section Name" categorisation and a "News Desk" categorisation of articles. In each, there are multiple overlaps. For example - an article can be considered as a "Foreign" category under News Desk and "World" category under Section Name. I created a combination of both of these and ranked people by number of citations each month across 4 categories - "Arts and Culture", "OpEd/Opinion", "World" and "Busines and Technology."
